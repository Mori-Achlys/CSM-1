About

This webpage is meant to give easy and useful advice on issues of mental health, general issues, or issues about making change and goals. This site will go into details of how to healthily handle problems and difficult situations in one's life. In the Mental illness help tab, it will provide more specific details on how to handle situations or emotions and impulses that arise in that field. In the General issues tab, It will provide assistance into more common problems, such as how to calm down or how to meditate and take care of yourself.

Disclamer

I am not a licenced professional and cannot give in-depth specific advice for serious situations. If you do require immediate assistance reach out to a health professional near you.
